Simulating black holes with Nmesh

I am performing simulations with single black hole puncture initial data using the new evolution system involving the conformal rescaling of the generalized harmonic gauge. Since this is a new system, it requires extensive testing. I have been performing simulations with the purpose of testing the robustness, stability, and the convergence of error with increasing resolution for this system.

Related publication: arXiv:2212.06340.
Related presentations: APS April 2021.
Project personel: L. Ji, A. Adhikari, W. Tichy.
