Fluctuation-dissipation relation in accelerated frames

Prior to my doctoral research, I worked on a project related to Unruh radiation (a thermal bath of uniform temperature), that an uniformly accelerated observer will see in the Rindler spacetime. Through the equivalence theoremâ€™s implication that an accelerated frame can mimic gravity, the properties of Unruh radiation also apply to Hawking radiation. In this project, we modeled the thermal radiation bath as a simple scalar field and showed that it can be treated with the same apparatus of non-equilibrium statistical mechanics related to the fluctuation-dissipation theorem governed by the Langevin equation that applies to other phenomena such as Brownian motion.

Related publication: arXiv:1707.01333.
