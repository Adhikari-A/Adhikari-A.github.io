Welcome

Hello, I am Ananya Adhikari, a PhD candidate in physics at the Florida Atlantic University (FAU), working under the supervision of Prof Wolfgang Tichy. My research focuses on computational general relativity and related astrophysical phenomena explored through it, such as neutron star mergers, gravitational wave signals, and more.

Curriculum Vitae: US | EU

Education

  • PhD in Physics, Florida Atlantic University (Ongoing)
    Thesis Advisor: Prof Wolfgang Tichy
  • MS in Physics, Florida Atlantic University(2018)
  • MS in Physics, Indian Institute of Technology(2016)
  • BS in Physics, Hooghly Mohsin College (The University of Burdwan)(2014)

Research Interests

  • Computational general relativity
  • Binary neutron star (BNS) inspiral and merger and related phenomena:
    • Gravitational wave signals
    • Remnant object properties, such as mass.
    • Tidal effects
  • Black hole evolutions
  • Multimessenger astrophysics
